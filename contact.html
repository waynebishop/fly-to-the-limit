Photography

We are very experienced and equipped to assist you with professional photograpic assignments and the doors can be removed from our helicopters if needed to get a clear unobstructed view.

If you are after a special location please let us know as with our vast combined local knowledge we can help you succeed.

Adventure

We are very experienced and equipped to assist you with any outdoor adventures and specialise in provision drops as well as transport for you and your gear to and from your destination. We can land on the lakes, snow and ice as well as suitable clearings in the bush.

If you are after a special location or activity please let us know as with our vast combined local knowledge, and contacts with local guides, we can help you succeed.
