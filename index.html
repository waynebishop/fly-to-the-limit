Welcome aboard!

Operating out of Queenstown and Wanaka in New Zealands' spectacular South Island, we offer travellers a range of breathtaking scenic flights in both fixed wing aircraft and helicopters, from high octane thrills in our stunt plane to the most peaceful flight experience possible in a glider floating high above the mountains and everything in between. We specialise in tailored one of a kind charters including delivery and pickup for trampers (hikers/bushwalkers), and also have a provisioning service for fresh supplies needed for great expeditions into the wilderness. If you are after an extreme experience in the most beautiful region on earth, you have come to the right place. Your adventure starts here!
